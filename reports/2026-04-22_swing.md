## Methodology

- **Entry**: last close on the scan date.
- **Stop**: `entry − 2 · ATR` (ATR from the 14-day range). A trade is
  invalidated if price closes below this level.
- **Target**: `min(entry + 3 · ATR, 52w high)` — aiming for a ~1.5 R:R trade
  capped at the prior high, which typically acts as resistance.
- **Not included here**: any name already near its 52-week high (late-stage
  trend concern), any death-cross structure, or any stock where ADX < 18
  (weak trend).
